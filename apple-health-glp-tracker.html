FAQ

Questions about Apple Health in GLPzy

Does GLPzy work with Apple Health?

Yes. Apple Health connection is optional and read-only, with weight history, height, calories, protein, water, and broader recent context available if you choose to connect it.

Does GLPzy write health records back into Apple Health?

No. In the current setup GLPzy reads Apple Health context but does not write data back to Apple Health.

Is Apple Health only for Premium?

No. Apple Health connection is available on the free plan.

Can I still use GLPzy without Apple Health?

Yes. GLPzy works in manual mode and does not require Apple Health to use core tracking.

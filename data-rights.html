Privacy choices and data rights

How the current Medensa app handles user choice.

1. No account requirement

The current app does not require an in-app account for core tracking. That means many requests can be handled directly on your device rather than through an account portal.

2. Local delete controls

You can remove local records using the delete-data controls in the app or by deleting the app from your device.

3. Apple Health permissions

Apple Health access is optional. You can manage Health permissions through Apple’s Health and system privacy settings. If you revoke access, Medensa should continue in manual mode.

4. Support correspondence

If you sent Medensa emails, screenshots, or exported files, you can contact sjgoodapps@gmail.com to request action on that correspondence, subject to any legal retention obligations.

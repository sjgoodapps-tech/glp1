Data Rights

Privacy choices and data rights

Last updated: 2026-03-30

This page explains the privacy choices and rights that may be available to you depending on your location and the law that applies. It should be read alongside the Privacy Policy.

1. Access

You may request confirmation of whether personal information about you is being handled and, where applicable, request a copy of that information, subject to lawful verification and any permitted exemptions.

2. Correction

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information. Some information can also be updated directly in the app.

3. Export

Where supported, you may export your own records from the app or request a copy of information in a structured format, subject to technical feasibility, verification, and legal limits.

4. Deletion

Medensa does not require an in-app account to get started. You can delete local records using the in-app delete-data controls and remove the app from your device. You can also request deletion of support correspondence or other information you shared directly.

5. Restriction and objection

Where applicable, you may request restriction of certain handling or object to certain handling based on your circumstances.

6. Withdrawal of consent

Where handling depends on consent, you may withdraw that consent. Withdrawal does not affect lawful handling that took place before it was withdrawn.

7. Apple Health permissions

Apple Health integration is optional. In the current customer-facing flow, Medensa reads weight history and height from Apple Health and presents the connection as import-only. Apple Health permissions can also be changed later in Apple’s Health and system settings.

8. Identity checks

Before some requests are completed, identity verification may be needed to protect users and prevent unauthorised disclosure.

9. Complaints

If you are dissatisfied with the outcome of a privacy request, you may contact Medensa again for review. Depending on your location, you may also have the right to contact the relevant supervisory authority or regulator.

10. Contact

Privacy requests can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

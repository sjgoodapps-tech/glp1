Data Rights

User privacy choices and data rights

Last updated: 2026-03-30

This page explains the privacy choices and data rights that may be available to you depending on your location and applicable law. It should be read together with the Privacy Policy.

1. Access

You may request confirmation of whether personal data about you is processed and, where applicable, request a copy of that data, subject to lawful verification and any permitted exemptions.

2. Correction

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. Some information may also be editable directly in the app.

3. Export

Where supported, you may export your own records or request a copy of data in a structured format, subject to verification, technical feasibility, and legal limitations.

4. Deletion

Medensa does not require an in-app account to get started. You can delete local records using the in-app delete-data controls. You may also request deletion of support correspondence or other information you have shared directly.

5. Restriction and objection

Where applicable, you may request restriction of certain processing or object to certain processing based on your circumstances.

6. Withdrawal of consent

Where processing depends on consent, you may withdraw that consent. Withdrawal does not affect lawful processing carried out before it took effect.

7. Apple Health permissions

Apple Health integration is optional. If you enable it, Medensa reads weight history and height from Apple Health. In the current setup, nothing is written back. Apple Health permissions can also be changed later through Apple’s Health and system settings.

8. Identity verification

Before fulfilling certain requests, identity verification may be required to protect users and prevent unauthorised disclosure.

9. Complaints

If you are dissatisfied with the outcome of a privacy request, you may contact Medensa again for review. Depending on your location, you may also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority or regulator.

10. Contact

Privacy requests can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

Data rights

Privacy choices and data rights

How Medensa handles user choice and local data control.

1. No account requirement

Core tracking does not require an in-app account. Many requests can therefore be handled directly on the device rather than through an account portal.

2. Local delete controls

Users can remove local records using delete-data controls in the app or by deleting the app from the device.

3. Apple Health permissions

Apple Health access is optional. Permissions can be managed in Apple Health and in iPhone privacy settings. If access is revoked, Medensa continues in manual mode.

4. Support correspondence

If a user has sent emails, screenshots, or exported files to Medensa support, they can contact sjgoodapps@gmail.com to request action on that correspondence, subject to any legal retention obligations.

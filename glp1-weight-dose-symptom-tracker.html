FAQ

Questions about GLPzy as a broader GLP-1 tracker

What makes GLPzy different from a simple dose log?

GLPzy combines dose logs with weight review, symptom tracking, photo milestones, Apple Health context, reminders, and local-first history in the same app.

Do I need an account?

No. GLPzy does not require a mandatory in-app account for core tracking.

Does GLPzy work with Apple Health?

Yes. Apple Health connection is optional, read-only, and available on the free plan.

Can I track custom or compounded routines?

Yes. GLPzy supports source-backed, custom, and compounded medication setups.

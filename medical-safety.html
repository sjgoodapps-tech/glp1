Medical Safety

Medical disclaimer and safety

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Core safety boundary. Medensa is a tracking and journaling service. It does not provide medical advice and must not be used to make medical or dosing decisions.

1. What Medensa is for

Medensa is designed to help users organise and review personal records such as medication logs, reminders, symptom entries, weight notes, wellness notes, and related routine information.

2. What Medensa is not for

Medensa is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, mitigate, or prevent disease. It is not intended to replace medical judgment or clinical advice.

3. No dosing guidance

You must not use Medensa to decide whether to start, stop, skip, delay, advance, increase, decrease, or otherwise change any medication, dose amount, dose timing, dosing interval, or treatment plan.

4. Charts, summaries, and estimates

Charts, reminders, summaries, and estimated views are informational only. They are not laboratory measurements, clinical assessments, or treatment recommendations.

5. Non-emergency use only

Medensa is not an emergency service and is not monitored for urgent health events. If you believe you may be experiencing a medical emergency, seek urgent medical help immediately.

6. Accuracy of user-entered information

The usefulness of logs, summaries, exports, or reminders depends on the accuracy of the information entered by the user and the functioning of the device, permissions, and any enabled integrations.

7. Clinician instructions prevail

If there is any difference between what you see in Medensa and the instructions given by your clinician, pharmacist, or the relevant product labelling, follow the clinician or product instructions.

8. Shared exports

Exports are user-directed tools for communication and record-keeping. They do not convert Medensa into a clinical system, prescribing tool, or medical advice service.

9. Contact

For product questions, contact sjgoodapps@gmail.com. Support cannot provide clinical guidance.

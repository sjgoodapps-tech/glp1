Medical Safety

Safety and product boundary

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Core boundary. Medensa is for personal tracking and review. Estimated medication level for personal tracking only. Not medical advice.

1. What Medensa is for

Medensa is designed to help you record doses, reminders, symptoms, weight, and routine history in one place so those records are easier to review later.

2. What Medensa is not for

Medensa must not be used as the basis for medical decisions, medication changes, or urgent care decisions.

3. Charts, summaries, and estimated views

Charts, reminders, summaries, and estimated medication level views are simplified informational tools based on the records available in the app. They are not direct measurements and must not replace clinician guidance.

4. Urgent situations

Medensa is not monitored for urgent events and is not an emergency service. If you believe you may need urgent medical help, contact your local emergency services or an urgent care provider.

5. Accuracy of records

The usefulness of reminders, summaries, and exports depends on the accuracy of the information you enter and on the permissions and settings enabled on your device.

6. Clinician guidance comes first

Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

7. Shared exports

Exports are for your own records and conversations. They do not turn Medensa into a clinical system or a source of clinician instructions.

8. Contact

For product questions, contact sjgoodapps@gmail.com. Support cannot provide clinical guidance.

Medical safety

Medical disclaimer and safety boundary

Explicit safety boundaries for Medensa.

Do not use Medensa for urgent care. The app is not an emergency service and must not be relied on for urgent medical decisions.

1. Tracking, not treatment

Medensa is designed to help users record and review personal history. It does not diagnose conditions, recommend treatment, or tell users how to change medication.

2. Estimated medication level

The estimated medication level view is for personal tracking only. It is not a blood concentration measurement, dose calculator, or clinical recommendation.

3. Clinician input

Users should speak with a clinician before making medical decisions, especially decisions about starting, stopping, delaying, increasing, decreasing, or skipping medication.

Deployment checklist

1. Replace placeholders in `site-config.js`, HTML canonical tags, Open Graph tags, `robots.txt`, and `sitemap.xml`.
2. Deploy the full folder contents, including `assets`, to the live site root.
3. Verify the site property in Google Search Console.
4. Submit `sitemap.xml` in Search Console.
5. Test `robots.txt` is reachable and correct.
6. Confirm there are no broken internal links.
7. Confirm App Store and TestFlight buttons hide when URLs are blank or placeholders, and show when real URLs are present.
8. Confirm the waitlist email CTA always opens correctly.

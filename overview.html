At a glance

Product position

  • Doserra is presented as a GLP-1 companion for tracking and review.
  • The site uses consumer-facing language rather than internal project wording.
  • The public landing page and the support pages are aligned in tone and scope.

Important boundaries

  • The service is for tracking, journaling, reminders, and review.
  • It is not presented as diagnosis, treatment, or dosing advice.
  • Questions about medical decisions should be directed to a clinician.

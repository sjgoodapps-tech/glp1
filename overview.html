Trust centre

Privacy, support, and product boundaries

Updated for the current Medensa app build.
Medensa is a personal tracking app. It helps users record and review medication-related history. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or dose instructions.

Current product behaviour

  • No account is required for core tracking.
  • Core records are stored locally on device.
  • Apple Health is optional. The current setup imports body weight history and height only.
  • Premium is offered through monthly and annual App Store subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases and delete-local-data controls are exposed in the app settings.

At a glance

Product position

  • Medensa is presented as an iPhone app for GLP-1 tracking and review.
  • The site uses consumer-facing language rather than internal project wording.
  • The landing page and trust pages are aligned in tone and scope.

Important boundaries

  • The service is for tracking, journaling, reminders, exports, and review.
  • It is not presented as diagnosis, treatment, or dosing advice.
  • Questions about medical decisions should be directed to a clinician.

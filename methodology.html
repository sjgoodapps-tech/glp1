Methodology

Methodology and limitations

High-level framing for charts and estimates in the current Medensa app.

1. Logged history

Weight, dose, symptom, note, photo, and order views are built from the records you log or import into the app.

2. Estimated medication level

The estimated medication level view is a relative tracking curve built from logged dose timing and a medication half-life configuration. It is designed to help you review timing patterns, not to act as a clinical concentration measurement.

3. Country-aware setup

The setup flow uses a bundled ruleset to filter country-specific obesity-focused medication options, presentations, and dose paths for the supported launch markets in the app.

4. Limits

Charts and summaries depend on the accuracy of what you log. They do not explain why something happened and should not be used as treatment advice.

Methodology

Methodology and limitations

How charts, history views, and estimates are framed in Medensa.

1. Logged history

Weight, dose, symptom, note, photo, and supply views are built from records that the user logs or imports into the app.

2. Estimated medication level

The estimated medication level view is a relative tracking curve based on logged dose timing and a medication half-life configuration. It is designed to help users review timing patterns, not to provide a clinical concentration measurement.

3. Country-aware setup

The setup flow uses a bundled ruleset to filter country-specific obesity-focused medication options, presentations, and dose paths for supported launch markets.

4. Limits

Charts and summaries depend on the accuracy of user-entered data. They do not explain why something happened and should not be used as treatment advice.

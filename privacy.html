Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Important. Medensa is designed as a local-first iPhone app. Health-related entries are stored on your device, and Medensa does not require an in-app account to get started.

1. Who operates Medensa

Medensa is operated by Steven Good in the United Kingdom. Privacy enquiries can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data may be processed when you use the Medensa website, the Medensa iPhone app, and related support channels.

3. Data categories

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Health and routine data you enterMedication name, dose amount, dose time, injection entry, weight, symptoms, notes, appetite or GI logs, reminders, photos, and supply recordsCore tracking, review, visualisation, reminders, and user-directed exports

4. How data is collected

  • Directly from you when you enter records, change settings, or contact support.
  • From Apple systems for subscription and entitlement information.
  • From Apple Health only if you choose to enable that connection.
  • From support materials you voluntarily provide, such as screenshots or device details.

5. How data is used

  • to provide the app’s logging, reminder, review, and export features;
  • to manage subscription entitlements made available through Apple systems;
  • to respond to support, safety, and privacy requests;
  • to maintain service reliability, error monitoring, abuse prevention, and security;
  • to meet legal, tax, accounting, and compliance obligations.

6. Health data and advertising

Some information handled by Medensa may be health-related personal data. Medensa does not sell personal data. Medensa does not use health data for targeted advertising, cross-context behavioural advertising, or advertising profiles.

7. Apple Health

Apple Health integration is optional. When enabled, Medensa reads weight history and height from Apple Health to support setup and tracking. In the current setup, Medensa does not write data back to Apple Health.

8. Disclosure of personal data

Medensa is designed so app records remain on your device unless you choose to export them or send information to support. Personal data may be disclosed where reasonably necessary to support the service, process subscriptions, comply with law, protect rights or safety, or respond to misuse.

9. Retention

10. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, delete, restrict, object to, or appeal certain processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent where consent applies. More detail is available on the Data Rights page.

11. Children

Medensa is not intended for children under 18.

12. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes to the service, legal requirements, or operational controls. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated date.

13. Contact

Privacy enquiries: sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

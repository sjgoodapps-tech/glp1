Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Summary. Medensa is designed as a local-first iPhone app. Core records are intended to stay on your device unless you choose to export them or send information to support.

1. Who runs Medensa

Medensa is operated by Steven Good in the United Kingdom. Privacy enquiries can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

2. What this policy covers

This policy explains how personal information may be handled when you use the Medensa website, the Medensa iPhone app, and related support channels.

3. Information Medensa may handle

CategoryExamplesWhy it is used
Records you enter in the appMedication name, dose, date, time, weight, symptoms, notes, reminders, and other routine entries you choose to keepTracking, reminders, review, summaries, and user-directed exports
Optional Apple Health dataWeight history and height if you choose to connect Apple HealthSetup support and ongoing weight tracking
Support correspondenceEmail address, screenshots, and details you decide to sendSupport, privacy requests, and follow-up

4. How information is collected

  • Directly from you when you use the app, update settings, or contact support.
  • From Apple systems for App Store subscription and entitlement information.
  • From Apple Health only if you choose to connect it.
  • From support material you voluntarily provide, such as screenshots or device details.

5. How information is used

  • to provide logging, reminder, review, and export features inside the app;
  • to manage Premium access made available through Apple systems;
  • to respond to support, safety, and privacy questions;
  • to keep the service reliable and secure;
  • to meet legal, tax, accounting, or compliance requirements where they apply.

6. Local-first handling and sharing

Medensa is designed so your app records stay on your device unless you choose to export them or share information with support. Medensa does not sell personal data and does not use health-related data for targeted advertising or advertising profiles.

7. Apple Health

Apple Health integration is optional. In the current customer-facing flow, Medensa reads weight history and height to support setup and tracking. The Apple Health connection is presented as import-only.

8. Retention

Data typeRetention approach
App recordsKept on your device until you delete them or remove the app, subject to your own device backup settings.
Support correspondenceKept only for as long as reasonably needed for support, security, legal, or compliance follow-up.
Diagnostics and security logsKept on a limited basis where needed for reliability and protection of the service.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, delete, restrict, object to, or appeal certain handling of your personal data, and to withdraw consent where consent applies. More detail is available on the Data Rights page.

10. Children

Medensa is not intended for children under 18.

11. Updates to this policy

This page may be updated if the service changes or if legal or operational requirements change. The latest version will be shown here with the updated date.

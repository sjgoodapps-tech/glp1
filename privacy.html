Privacy policy

Applies to the current Medensa iPhone app and these public trust pages.
Core product position. Medensa is local-first for core tracking. No in-app account is required in the current app.

1. Who publishes Medensa

Medensa is published by Steven Good in the United Kingdom. For privacy or support questions, contact sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

2. What data the current app handles

Data typeExamplesCurrent use
On-device tracking recordsDose events, weight entries, symptom logs, notes, progress photos, supply-order records, reminders, settingsCore app functionality, history review, charts, reminders, and exports
Optional Apple Health dataBody weight history and heightImported only when you choose to connect Apple Health. The current setup is read-only.
Subscription and entitlement dataSubscription status, purchase and restore events made available through ApplePremium access management and billing support context
Support correspondenceEmail messages, screenshots, and files you choose to sendTechnical support, billing help, and privacy requests

3. What stays on device

In the current product, core tracking records are intended to stay on your device unless you choose to export them, share them, or include them in a support request.

4. Apple Health

Apple Health access is optional. The current app describes the integration as importing body weight history and height only. It also states that nothing is written back in the current setup.

If you do not grant Apple Health access, Medensa still works in manual mode.

5. Sharing and sale of data

Medensa is not marketed as an advertising product. The current trust position for the app is that health-related information is not sold and is not used for targeted advertising.

6. Retention

Data typeRetention approach
On-device tracking recordsKept on your device until you delete records, use the delete-local-data controls, or remove the app.
Support correspondenceKept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, resolve the issue, and meet any legal or accounting obligations.
Subscription support contextKept as needed for billing support, refund handling, accounting, and dispute resolution.

7. Your choices

  • Use the app without creating an in-app account.
  • Decline Apple Health and continue in manual mode.
  • Delete local records inside the app.
  • Contact support about correspondence you have sent to Medensa.

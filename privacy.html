Privacy

Privacy policy

Applies to the Medensa iPhone app and these public trust pages.

Core privacy position. Medensa is local-first for core tracking. No in-app account is required for basic use.

1. Publisher

Medensa is published by Steven Good in the United Kingdom. Privacy and support questions can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

2. Data handled by Medensa

On-device tracking records

Dose events, weight entries, symptom logs, notes, progress photos, supply-order records, reminders, and settings. These support logging, history review, charts, reminders, and exports.

Optional Apple Health data

Body weight history and height. These are imported only when the user chooses to connect Apple Health. Medensa does not write data back to Apple Health.

Subscription and entitlement data

Subscription status, purchase events, and restore events made available through Apple for Premium access management and billing support context.

Support correspondence

Email messages, screenshots, and files that a user chooses to send for technical support, billing help, or privacy requests.

3. What stays on the device

Core tracking records remain on the device unless the user exports them, shares them, or includes them in a support request.

4. Apple Health

Apple Health access is optional. Medensa reads body weight history and height from Apple Health when the user chooses to connect it. Medensa does not write data back to Apple Health.

If Apple Health access is not granted, Medensa continues to work in manual mode.

5. Advertising and sale of data

Medensa is not an advertising product. Health-related information is not sold and is not used for targeted advertising.

6. Retention

On-device tracking records

Kept on the device until the user deletes records, uses delete-local-data controls, or removes the app.

Support correspondence

Kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, resolve the issue, and meet legal or accounting obligations.

Subscription support context

Kept as needed for billing support, refund handling, accounting, and dispute resolution.

7. User choices

  • Use the app without creating an in-app account.
  • Decline Apple Health and continue in manual mode.
  • Delete local records inside the app.
  • Contact support about correspondence already sent to Medensa.

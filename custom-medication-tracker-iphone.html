FAQ

Questions about custom medication tracking in GLPzy

Can GLPzy track a custom medication name?

Yes. GLPzy supports custom medication setup for personal tracking.

Can I choose my own route and schedule?

Yes. Route, presentation, dose, and daily, weekly, or every-N-days schedules are part of the custom setup flow.

Can I still connect Apple Health?

Yes. Apple Health connection remains optional and read-only regardless of the medication setup type.

Is this meant for clinical guidance?

No. GLPzy is a personal tracking and review app only.

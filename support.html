Support

Support

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Medical safety notice. Medensa is not an emergency service and must not be used to make urgent medical decisions. If you believe you may be experiencing a medical emergency, seek urgent medical care immediately.

1. Contact

Support, privacy, and general product questions can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

2. What support can help with

  • restore-purchase issues and subscription entitlement questions;
  • billing support context and renewal questions;
  • bug reports, crash reports, and device compatibility issues;
  • export, deletion, and privacy-rights requests;
  • accessibility issues and product usability problems.

3. What support cannot provide

Support cannot provide medical advice, dosing guidance, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or instructions on whether to start, stop, skip, delay, increase, or decrease any medication.

4. Helpful details to include

  • your device model and iOS version;
  • the app version;
  • a short description of the issue and what led to it;
  • whether the issue affects reminders, exports, subscriptions, Apple Health, or review screens;
  • screenshots only if you are comfortable sharing them.

5. Subscriptions and billing

Paid plans are offered through the Apple App Store and are subject to Apple’s billing and renewal systems. Subscription management, cancellation, and refund availability depend on Apple’s policies and your Apple account settings.

Medensa offers optional auto-renewing monthly and annual subscriptions for Premium features.

6. Privacy and deletion

Medensa does not require an in-app account to get started. Local app data can be deleted from within the app. Privacy, access, export, and deletion requests relating to information you shared with support can also be sent to the contact address above.

7. Accessibility

If you encounter an accessibility barrier while using Medensa, get in touch with enough detail for the issue to be investigated properly.

8. Service notices

Where appropriate, service updates may be communicated through in-app notices, email, or website updates.

9. Response times

Support and privacy requests are handled as quickly as reasonably possible. More complex requests may take longer where verification or legal review is required.

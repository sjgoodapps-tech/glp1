Support

Support

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Important. Medensa is not an emergency service. If you believe you may need urgent medical help, contact your local emergency services or an urgent care provider straight away.

1. How to contact support

Support, privacy, and general product questions can be sent to sjgoodapps@gmail.com.

2. What support can help with

  • restore-purchase issues and Premium entitlement questions;
  • billing context and renewal questions;
  • bug reports, crashes, and device compatibility problems;
  • export, deletion, and privacy requests;
  • accessibility barriers and product usability issues.

3. What support cannot do

Support cannot give medical advice, tell you how to change medication, or help you make dosing decisions. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

4. Helpful details to include

  • your device model and iOS version;
  • the app version;
  • a short description of what happened and what led to it;
  • whether the issue affects reminders, Apple Health, review screens, exports, or purchases;
  • screenshots only if you are comfortable sharing them.

5. Subscriptions and billing

Premium plans are offered through the Apple App Store and use Apple’s billing and renewal systems. Subscription management, cancellation, and refund availability depend on Apple’s policies and your Apple account settings.

6. Privacy and deletion

Medensa does not require an in-app account to get started. Local app data can be deleted from within the app. Requests relating to support correspondence or privacy questions can be sent to the contact address above.

7. Accessibility

If you hit an accessibility barrier while using Medensa, contact support with enough detail for the issue to be reviewed properly.

8. Response times

Support and privacy questions are handled as quickly as reasonably possible. Some requests can take longer if verification or extra review is required.

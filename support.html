Support

Technical, billing, and privacy support for Medensa.

Not for emergencies. Medensa support cannot provide urgent medical help. If you think you may be having a medical emergency, seek urgent medical care immediately.

1. Contact

Email sjgoodapps@gmail.com for technical issues, privacy questions, or billing support.

2. What support can help with

  • Restore-purchase issues and subscription entitlement questions.
  • Apple Health connection and manual-mode questions.
  • Reminder behaviour and local data deletion.
  • Import and export problems.
  • Bug reports, crashes, and device compatibility issues.

3. What support cannot do

Support does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or instructions about whether to start, stop, delay, increase, decrease, or skip medication.

4. Subscriptions

Medensa Premium is offered through monthly and annual auto-renewing App Store subscriptions. Purchase management, cancellation, and refunds remain subject to Apple’s systems and policies.

5. Helpful information to include

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • App version.
  • A short description of the issue and how to reproduce it.
  • Whether the issue affects reminders, Apple Health, imports, exports, or purchases.

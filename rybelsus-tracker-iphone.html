FAQ

Questions about tracking Rybelsus in GLPzy

Can GLPzy track Rybelsus tablet routines?

Yes. GLPzy supports personal tracking for Rybelsus with daily reminders, routine history, and local-first review tools.

Can I still use Apple Health with a tablet routine?

Yes. Apple Health connection is optional and remains read-only, adding weight and broader recent context alongside your manual routine history.

Does GLPzy only work for injections?

No. GLPzy supports oral tablet and pen-based routines, plus compounded and custom medication setups.

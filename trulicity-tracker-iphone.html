FAQ

Questions about tracking Trulicity in GLPzy

Can GLPzy track Trulicity routines?

Yes. GLPzy supports weekly personal tracking with dose logs, reminders, history, and review tools.

Can I review photos and weight together?

Yes. GLPzy combines photo milestones, weight review, and history in the same iPhone and iPad app.

Does Apple Health cost extra?

No. Apple Health connection is optional and available on the free plan in read-only mode.

Can I keep the data local?

Yes. GLPzy is designed as a local-first app with exports and backups available when you want them.

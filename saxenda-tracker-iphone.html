FAQ

Questions about tracking Saxenda in GLPzy

Can GLPzy track Saxenda routines?

Yes. GLPzy supports personal tracking for Saxenda with daily reminders, notes, photos, history, and review tools.

Can I compare progress photos in the app?

Yes. GLPzy includes photo milestones and before/after comparison tools, including a slider-based comparison flow.

Does GLPzy include Apple Health context?

Yes. Apple Health connection is optional and read-only, with weight history and broader recent context available if you choose to connect it.

Is GLPzy a medical advice app?

No. GLPzy is a personal tracking and review app only.

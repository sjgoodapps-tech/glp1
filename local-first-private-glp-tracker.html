FAQ

Questions about privacy and local-first use

Do I need an account to use GLPzy?

No. GLPzy does not require a mandatory in-app account for core tracking.

Where are my records stored?

GLPzy is designed as a local-first app, with records kept on the device unless you choose to export, share, or restore from a local backup file.

Can I still use Apple Health?

Yes. Apple Health connection is optional and read-only, and it does not change the local-first posture of the core app.

Where can I read the full privacy details?

Use the privacy, support, data-rights, methodology, and medical-safety pages linked from this site.

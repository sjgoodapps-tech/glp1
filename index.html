Trust centre

Privacy, support, and product boundaries

Applies to the Medensa iPhone app and these public trust pages.

Medensa is a personal tracking app for GLP-1 routines and related records. It helps users log doses, weight, symptoms, notes, progress photos, reminders, and supply orders in one place.

Medensa does not diagnose, prescribe, recommend treatment, or tell users what dose to take.

What Medensa does

  • No in-app account is required for core tracking.
  • Core records stay on the device unless the user exports or shares them.
  • Apple Health is optional and limited to read-only import of body weight history and height.
  • Premium is offered through monthly and annual auto-renewing App Store subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases and delete-local-data controls are available in Settings.

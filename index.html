FAQ

Frequently asked questions

These answers are written for end users and kept aligned with the app’s current scope.

What is Medensa?

Medensa is an iPhone app for tracking GLP-1 routines in one place. It helps you log doses, weight, symptoms, reminders, notes, and schedule changes, then review that history more clearly over time.

Which routines does Medensa support?

Medensa is built for both weekly and daily routines, including injection and oral setups where they apply to your treatment plan.

What can I log in the app?

You can log doses, weight, symptoms, notes, appetite or food-noise observations, GI notes, progress photos, and supply or order history.

Do I need an account?

No. Medensa does not require an in-app account to get started.

Does Medensa connect to Apple Health?

Yes. Apple Health integration is optional. When enabled, Medensa reads weight history and height from Apple Health. In the current setup, nothing is written back. Apple Health import includes a 14-day free access window from first connect, then requires Premium.

Can I export my history?

Yes. You can import a dose-history CSV, and with Premium you can export your history as CSV, JSON, or PDF for your own records.

What does Premium include?

Premium adds estimated medication level views, expanded review surfaces, calendar review, Apple Health import after the included access window, and export-ready records.

Is Medensa medical advice?

No. Medensa is for tracking, organisation, and review. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or tell you how to change treatment. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

Which platforms are supported?

Medensa is currently designed for iPhone.

Where can I read the privacy and support information?

The public site includes linked pages for privacy, support, terms, data rights, medical safety, and methodology so the app and the website tell the same story.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

These answers are written to support the product story without drifting outside the app’s published medical boundaries.

What is Doserra?

Doserra is a GLP-1 companion app for tracking doses, weight, symptoms, reminders, injection history, notes, and routine changes in one place. It is designed for personal organisation and review.

Which routines does Doserra support?

Doserra is designed around major GLP-1 and related weight-management routines, including weekly injections, daily routines, and oral tracking where relevant to the user’s setup.

What can I log in the app?

You can log doses, weight, symptoms, notes, nutrition, appetite or food-noise observations, GI notes, progress photos, and supply or order history.

Can Doserra remind me about upcoming doses?

Yes. Reminder and schedule tools are built into the product so you can keep track of upcoming doses, missed entries, and changes to your routine.

Does Doserra help with injection-site organisation?

Yes. The app includes body-map and site-history tools so you can review your own prior records and keep injections organised over time.

What does Plus add?

Plus adds deeper review tools rather than basic access, including estimated medication level views, advanced summaries, widgets, exports, and richer planning tools.

Is Doserra medical advice?

No. Doserra is for tracking, organisation, and review. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or tell you how to change treatment. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

How do the website and policy pages fit together?

The public site is structured as a sales page at the front with linked privacy, support, terms, data-rights, medical-safety, and methodology pages underneath it. Those pages explain how the product is presented and supported.

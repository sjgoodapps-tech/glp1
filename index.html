What the current app actually does

This page is aligned to the current repo and avoids promising features that are not clearly present in the app today.

01

Country-aware medication setup

Choose your country first and Medensa filters obesity-focused medication options, presentations, and dose paths for the supported launch markets in the app.

02

Fast daily logging

Log doses, weight, symptoms, notes, progress photos, and supply orders without turning the app into a spreadsheet.

03

Results built for review, not hype

Review weight trend, dose timeline, symptom overlays, and goal progress. Premium adds the estimated medication level view and deeper review surfaces.

04

Calendar history and editing

Review what was logged on a given day, edit older entries, and import dose-history CSV files when you are migrating into the app.

05

Optional Apple Health import

Connect Apple Health to import body weight history and height. In the current setup Medensa stays read-only. The app works fine without Apple Health.

06

Export and control

Restore purchases, adjust units and reminders, delete local data, and export full history as CSV, JSON, or PDF with Premium.

Built around conservative health copy

Medensa is framed as personal tracking. It does not give medical advice, it does not calculate a dose for you, and it should not be used to make treatment decisions.

What Medensa is for

  • Tracking what you took and when you took it
  • Reviewing weight and symptom history over time
  • Keeping reminders, notes, and routine records organised
  • Preparing a cleaner history for your own records or clinician discussions

What Medensa is not for

  • Diagnosis or treatment
  • Drug dosage calculation or optimisation
  • Emergency or urgent medical use
  • Promises about outcomes, response, or side-effect reduction

Frequently asked questions

The FAQ below is aligned to the current app behaviour and the current trust pages.

What is Medensa?
Medensa is an iPhone app for tracking GLP-1 medication routines and related records. The current app focuses on country-aware setup, logging, reminders, history review, and Premium review tools.
Does Medensa give medical advice?
No. Medensa is a personal tracking app. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or tell you how to change your dose. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.
Do I need an account to use it?
No. The current app is designed so core tracking can be used without creating an account.
What can I log in the current app?
The current build supports dose logs, weight entries, symptom logs, notes, progress photos, and supply-order tracking, alongside reminders and history review.
Does Medensa support pills as well as injections?
Yes. The app’s setup flow supports both injection and oral routes, with country-aware medication options shown for supported launch markets.
How does Apple Health work here?
Apple Health is optional. In the current setup Medensa imports body weight history and height only. The app states that nothing is written back in the current setup.
Is Apple Health included in Premium?
Apple Health import is treated as a Premium feature in the current app, with a 14-day access window from first connect before Premium is required.
What does Premium unlock?
The current Premium positioning includes the estimated medication level view, fuller calendar review, Apple Health after the access window, progress photo timeline review, supply reminders, and export tools.
Can I export my history?
Yes. The current app supports free CSV import and Premium exports for CSV, JSON, and PDF.
Which medications and countries are supported?
The app uses a bundled country ruleset to filter supported obesity-focused brands and dose paths for the current launch markets. Exact options depend on the country you choose during setup.

Implementation note. This pack is written for a subscription-based GLP-1 tracking app that handles sensitive health-related data. It is designed to look credible, premium, and operationally mature, with consistent language across privacy, support, subscription terms, user rights, medical boundaries, and methodology.

This policy centre is ready to publish on a GitHub Pages site. It is drafted to read like it came from a coordinated product, legal, compliance, and support workflow rather than from a prototype build.

Publishing notes

App Store metadata

Use privacy.html for the Privacy Policy URL, support.html for the Support URL, and terms.html for the Terms of Use link shown in the app and in subscription messaging.

Operational alignment

Make sure the app’s onboarding, paywall, settings, and account-deletion flow use the same medical boundary language and data-rights descriptions used in this pack.

Recommended next edits

  1. Decide the final brand and legal entity details.
  2. Decide whether the service is UK-only or available in the UK and US from launch.
  3. Set actual retention periods for support, diagnostics, and backups.
  4. Decide whether you will store health data only on device, or also sync to your backend.
  5. Decide whether the app will show estimated medication levels or only raw logs and trends.

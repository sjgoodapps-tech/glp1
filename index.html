FAQ

Questions people are likely to ask before downloading

What is GLPzy?

GLPzy is an iPhone app for tracking GLP-1 routines. It helps you record doses, weight, symptoms, reminders, and routine history in one calmer place.

Do I need an account?

No in-app account is required to get started.

Does GLPzy work with Apple Health?

Yes, optionally. In the current customer-facing flow, GLPzy reads weight history and height from Apple Health and treats the connection as import-only.

What stays free?

Medication setup, dose logging, weight logging, symptom logging, richer charts, estimated medication level, basic reminders, and core history remain in the free tier.

What does Premium add?

Premium adds advanced summaries, widgets, progress photos, full-history export, reorder reminders, deeper planning, and richer review surfaces.

Is GLPzy medical advice?

No. GLPzy is for personal tracking and review. Estimated medication level for personal tracking only. Not medical advice. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

Can I read the policy pages before using the app?

Yes. The site includes public pages for privacy, support, terms, data rights, medical safety, and methodology.

FAQ

Questions people are likely to ask before downloading

What is Medensa?

Medensa is an iPhone app for tracking GLP-1 routines. It helps you record doses, weight, symptoms, reminders, and routine history in one calmer place.

Do I need an account?

No in-app account is required to get started.

Does Medensa work with Apple Health?

Yes, optionally. In the current customer-facing flow, Medensa reads weight history and height from Apple Health and treats the connection as import-only.

What stays free?

Medication setup, dose logging, weight logging, symptom logging, basic reminders, core history, and basic charts and calendar review remain in the free tier.

What does Premium add?

Premium adds estimated medication level, advanced summaries, widgets, progress photos, full-history export, reorder reminders, deeper planning, and richer review surfaces.

Is Medensa medical advice?

No. Medensa is for personal tracking and review. Estimated medication level for personal tracking only. Not medical advice. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

Can I read the policy pages before using the app?

Yes. The site includes public pages for privacy, support, terms, data rights, medical safety, and methodology.

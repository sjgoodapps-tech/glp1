FAQ

Questions people are likely to ask before downloading

What is GLPzy?

GLPzy is an iPhone and iPad app for tracking GLP-1 routines. It helps you record doses, weight, symptoms, reminders, and routine history in one calmer place.

Do I need an account?

No in-app account is required to get started.

Does GLPzy work with Apple Health?

Yes, optionally. GLPzy can read weight history, height, body composition, sleep, movement, workouts, calories, protein, and water summaries from Apple Health in read-only mode for Today, Insights, and Settings.

Can I track custom or compounded treatments?

Yes. GLPzy includes supported medication selections, plus custom and compounded profiles for personal tracking.

What stays free?

Medication setup, dose logging, weight logging, symptom logging, nutrition logging, richer charts, the historical Estimated Exposure Trend overlay, basic reminders, core history, and free photo comparison tools remain in the free tier.

What does Premium add?

Premium adds projected Estimated Exposure Trend review, advanced summaries, widgets, full-history export, past and future month calendar browsing, body-map injection-site review, unlimited photo uploads, extra before / after styles, reorder reminders, and longer-range planning tools.

Where can I find the app's medical references?

GLPzy includes in-app medical references from Settings and directly from BMI and Estimated Exposure Trend source buttons, with matching bibliography pages on this site.

Is GLPzy medical advice?

No. GLPzy is for personal tracking and review. Estimated Exposure Trend is a relative personal-tracking estimate. It is not a measured blood concentration. It is based on a simplified model. Do not use this for dosing decisions. Always check with your clinician before making medical decisions.

Can I read the policy pages before using the app?

Yes. The site includes public pages for privacy, support, legal and trader information, data rights, medical safety, and methodology.

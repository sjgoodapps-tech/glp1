FAQ

Questions about tracking Victoza in GLPzy

Can GLPzy track Victoza pen routines?

Yes. GLPzy supports personal tracking for Victoza routines with daily reminders, history, notes, and review tools.

Is GLPzy good for daily logging?

Yes. Daily, weekly, and every-N-days schedules are part of the current treatment setup.

Can I add Apple Health context?

Yes. Apple Health connection is optional and read-only, with weight and broader recent context available if you choose to connect it.

Can I switch to a custom or compounded routine later?

Yes. GLPzy also supports custom and compounded medication setups if you want a more flexible personal record.
